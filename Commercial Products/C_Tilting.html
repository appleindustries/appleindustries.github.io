Tilting Wet-Grinder

Description

  • User friendly
  • Par of cylindrical rollers rotates clockwise and anti
    clockwise direction ensures faster grinding
  • Roller are housed in Stainless steel pipes with high tension
    spring inside ensures uniform pressures on the grinding
    medium
  • Unique grinding Technology ensures additional yield
    compared to conventional grinders.
  • Minimal in noise and Maximum in perforrmance
  • Tilting lock system holds the grinder in vertical position
    while grinding and in tilted position Draining the batter.
  • Top arm lock system Ensure to hold the stone assembly
    in position while grinding and cleaning.
  • FRP lid ensure hygiene.
  • Hardend Reduction gear system (worm type)
    Eensures smooth grinding.
  • Entire System is Mounted on the Powder coated
    M.S Channals.
  • Fully Stainless steel version is also Available.
